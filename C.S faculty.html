Computer Science Department

In the present world scenario computer science and engineering has its place in every aspect. It is the scientific and practical approach to computation and its applications. A computer engineer specializes in the theory of computation and the design of computational systems. It has totally changed the life style of people. It is the basic part for every engineering related aspects. Computer science engineering is one of the most well known and popular discipline of all engineering branches. The programming concepts will be very easy when one understands with a little intellectual thinking. At Jain Polytechnic we are preparing our students for real world challenges and encourage them to come up with ideas on their own and the department provides all the facilities required to materialize those ideas into actual projects.
